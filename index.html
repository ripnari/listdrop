03 — Embed anywhere

Your audience, your listings.

Wherever your buyers are reading, browsing, or following — ListDrop widgets live there too.

✍️ Substack / Newsletter
Your weekly drop, embedded.
Readers can bid directly inside the email preview or on the web version. No leaving, no friction.
<!-- paste in your Substack post -->
<script
 src="https://listdrop.io/w.js"
 data-id="ld_x9f2a1"></script>
📸 Link in Bio / Social
Turn your bio into a storefront.
Works with Linktree, Beacons, Carrd — or your own page. Your followers bid without switching apps.
<!-- any HTML page -->
<div id="listdrop-root"></div>
<script src="https://listdrop.io/w.js">
📝 Blog / Review Site
Content that closes.
Write the review. Embed the listing. Buyers who trust your taste buy on the spot. Monetize your credibility.
<!-- WordPress, Squarespace, Ghost -->
<script
 src="https://listdrop.io/w.js"
 data-theme="auto"></script>
